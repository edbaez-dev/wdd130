Los Dientes del Diablo

Experience one of the most challenging and thrilling rapids in the region. This expert-level trip takes you through Class IV and V rapids with breathtaking scenery and heart-pounding excitement.

  • Duration: Full Day (8 hours)
  • Difficulty: Expert
  • Price: $150 per person
  • Min. Age: 18 years
  • Group Size: 6-12 people

Big Mallard

The Big Mallard offers an incredible mix of challenging rapids and calm stretches perfect for intermediate rafters. Enjoy stunning river canyon views while navigating exciting Class III and IV rapids.

  • Duration: Full Day (6 hours)
  • Difficulty: Intermediate
  • Price: $120 per person
  • Min. Age: 14 years
  • Group Size: 4-12 people

Half Day Adventure

Perfect for beginners and families! This fun and accessible trip introduces you to the joy of white water rafting with Class II and III rapids. No experience necessary — just a sense of adventure!

  • Duration: Half Day (3 hours)
  • Difficulty: Beginner
  • Price: $75 per person
  • Min. Age: 8 years
  • Group Size: 4-16 people
